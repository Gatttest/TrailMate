The TrailMap feature allows you to locate your position over the trail.

It shows km markers to guide trailwalkers about the distance covered
Trail StartingPoint, Checkpoints and FinishingPoint are clearly marked on the map.
The Location Icon when clicked, show your current location on track. Just press it and wait for your current location to appear on map.
Hide Checkpoint markers by clicking on small EYE icon.
Zoom-in and Zoom-out by clicking +/- buttons.
Share You Progress with your friends by clicking on "Share Button" and selecting your favourite social media app like Whatsapp, Facebook etc.
The SOS icon on top can be clicked in case you need an urgent help from Oxfam India staff or security personals.
During night, clicking on Flash icon will switch ON your camera flashlight. Switch Off by clicking it again.

CheckPoint Menu:
This feature displays all the important places on Trail eg. STARTINGPOINT, CHECKPOINTS and FINISHINGPOINT.
Click on any Point-Of-Interest(POI) and view the facilities provided by OXFAM India for you.

Information Screen
On every screen, clicking on Information Icon (Bottom - Right) will display contact information for Oxfam Coordinators present on each checkpoint.

Weather screen displays current weather on the Trail Location.

Share your expreriance and leave your valuable feedback from Feedback Screen.

Clicking on Exit button will close the app completely and your location will not be shared with Oxfam India.
